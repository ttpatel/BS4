GUJARATI

Gujarati cuisine is that of the state of Gujarat, in western India. The typical Gujarati thali consists of rotli, dal or kadhi, rice, and shaak.

PUNJABI

Punjabi cuisine is known for its rich, buttery flavours along with the extensive vegetarian and meat dishes.

Mexican food so irresistible is that it is a blend of different cultures & distinct blend of spices,seasonings & vibrant colors.

ITALIAN

Italian cuisine uses ingredients such as tomatoes,meat,fish & pecorino cheese. Italian cuisine is well known for diverse variety of pasta.
